<h1 align="left">05-Project-Library-Management-System</h1>

###

<p align="left">The Library Item Management System is developed to provide a centralized and automated platform for managing borrowing transactions and tracking both physical and digital resources within an academic library. The system aims to enhance operational efficiency, accuracy of records, and accessibility of information for both librarians and borrowers.</p>
